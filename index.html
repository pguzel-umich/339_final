Shifting Perspectives: Rationale Behind the Pet Finder Platform

My rationale behind this website was simple; I wanted to create a site/app where pet owners can connect with others who share similar interests and experiences, because, after all, pets are often a reflection of their owners.
The initial concept went through a lot throughout the development process to align better with academic requirements and ethical considerations.

Initially, I wanted to create a dating platform for pets, which I later plan do later as a personal project. However, after contemplating the ethical harms of over-breeding, I became concerned about the implications of making a dating site for animals. After discussing this topic with my girlfriend (whose family's pets are featured in my site), I decided to shift the topic from a Tinder for pets to a site where pets can make pals.

Note: my girlfriend is not in this class only provided me with information on breeding ethics in the United States.
As a result, I decided to shift the focus and design a platform where pet owners can meet other pet owners, arrange playdates, and find suitable playmates for their pets.

While the initial idea of a "pet dating site" has changed quite a bit, I believe the end result is still quite good and can serve as a improvement when I am working on this idea as a personal project. This kind of friendly approach would also be fun to improve upon the initial dating site idea, having both of these mixed up could offer a much better product.
